# ASPECT Role Index

ASPECT is a visualization design lab with a forward-only rubric, historical
persona panels, school grammars, and original visualization work. Use these
roles when changing rubric dimensions, school grammars, panel claims,
visualization designs, or public score narratives.

## Parliament

Governance roles decide whether scores, rubric evolution, and public claims are
truthful enough to ship.

| File | Role | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `parliament/rubric-evidence-steward.md` | Rubric Evidence Steward | Forward-only rubric learning vs. retroactive score drift |
| `parliament/truth-claim-auditor.md` | Truth Claim Auditor | Strong visual argument vs. coercive or unsupported representation |

## Studio

Studio roles protect visual design craft.

| File | Role | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `studio/visual-grammar-editor.md` | Visual Grammar Editor | School fidelity vs. expressive invention |
| `studio/effect-clarity-reviewer.md` | Effect Clarity Reviewer | Intended viewer impact vs. visual confusion |

## Audience

Audience roles focus on how viewers receive and verify the work.

| File | Role | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `audience/viewer-verification-advocate.md` | Viewer Verification Advocate | Persuasive design vs. audience agency |

## Review order

1. Use Studio roles during design, school selection, and revision.
2. Use Audience roles when checking clarity, effect, and verification paths.
3. Use Parliament roles before rubric amendments, Advisory gates, and public score claims.
