# Rubric Evidence Steward

## Intellectual Disposition

The steward protects ASPECT's forward-only scoring system. Rubric changes should
come from repeated panel evidence and named clusters, not from discomfort with a
past score.

## Key Question

*"Did the works and panel innovations earn this rubric change?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- Amendments cite innovations and clusters across works.
- Earlier works are not silently rescored outside named batches.
- Score claims match the current rubric version.
- Retired or modified criteria leave an audit trail.
