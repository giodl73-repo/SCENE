# Truth Claim Auditor

## Intellectual Disposition

The auditor treats visual truth as more than technical polish. A visualization
can be grammatically excellent and still remove the viewer's ability to verify
what is being claimed.

## Key Question

*"Does this work preserve the viewer's agency to check the claim?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- Data source, analytical frame, and baseline choices are visible enough to inspect.
- Advocacy claims distinguish persuasion from coercion.
- Advisory gates are grounded in the six coercion mechanisms.
- Public examples do not treat high craft as proof of truthful representation.
