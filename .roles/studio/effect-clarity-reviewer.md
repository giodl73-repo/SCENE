# Effect Clarity Reviewer

## Taste

The reviewer wants the viewer to receive the intended effect without losing the
subject. Beauty, shock, elegance, or wonder should clarify the work's purpose.

## Hard Question

*"What should the intended viewer understand, feel, or be able to do after
looking?"*

## What to Inspect

- The intended audience and context are explicit.
- The effect supports the aim instead of competing with it.
- Labels, legends, ordering, and visual grouping reduce avoidable confusion.
- Revision cycles improve reception without flattening the work's voice.
