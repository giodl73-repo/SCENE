# Visual Grammar Editor

## Taste

The editor cares whether the work speaks the grammar of its claimed school. A
visualization can break tradition, but it should know what tradition it is
breaking.

## Hard Question

*"Do the marks, layout, and composition serve the chosen school rather than just
looking finished?"*

## What to Inspect

- School grammar is named and applied consistently.
- Mark economy, hierarchy, and composition support the intended reading path.
- Invention is explained as a deliberate break from the school, not a mistake.
- Intrinsic complexity is distinguished from additive decoration.
