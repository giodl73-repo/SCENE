### Key Findings from Batch 0041-0050

**1. Beat-92 achieved:** Work 0041 scored 92.7 — the design that optimized for every v2.2 dimension simultaneously reached its target. The score is attributable to: perfect school-story match, gold-standard data, no decorative marks, high-stakes advocacy with honest gap acknowledgment, and audience well-matched to context.

**2. Proof elegance is a real scoring variable:** The Euclid (86.7) vs. Chu-pei (93.6) comparison demonstrates that simpler proofs with smaller foundation requirements score higher through the E-pathway (E1, Clarity, Execution Economy). Innovation #275 formalizes this.

**3. The three Minard works confirm rubric calibration:** Napoleon (85.5), Hannibal (85.1), Wine (84.4) are separated by at most 1.1 points on the technical dimensions. The gaps are explained by: data quality differences (Napoleon contemporary vs. Hannibal ancient sources), dimensional completeness (Napoleon two-register vs. others one-register), and Aim/Effect stakes (tragedy vs. commercial data). Emotional weight does not inflate scores.

**4. ADVISORY score (57.1) reflects genuine failure:** Work 0049 (WWI propaganda) is the project low, below the 60-point binding threshold. The Integrity (4.75/20) and Purpose (3.9/15) scores reflect structural failures: no data, manipulative mechanism, advocacy-as-coercion. Comparable to HOLC (69.2) but lower because the propaganda poster contains no data at all and the manipulative mechanism is intentional.

**5. Absence-as-encoding is a legitimate technique:** Works 0045 (language fade) and 0047 (Amazon deforestation) both use visual near-absence or complete absence to represent real-world loss. The technique is legitimate when marks are present-but-dimmed (not deleted), when opacity encodes a real data variable, and when a legend is present. Innovation #276.

**6. W cluster extended to degraded ecosystems:** Innovation #278 shows that ecology-as-composition applies to the full spectrum of ecological states, not just intact ecosystems. The visual density of a depleted ecosystem (nearly empty cattle pasture panel) is as much an ecological argument as the density of an intact one.
